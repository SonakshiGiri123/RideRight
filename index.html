Assess Your Reaction Speed

Please tap the screen immediately when the display turns green. We will conduct five rounds to determine your average response time.

Alcohol often slows your reaction time before you feel noticeably impaired. Even at a 0.05% BAC, reaction times can increase by up to 40ms. This small delay can significantly impact your ability to stop safely in traffic.

A typical sober baseline is between 200ms and 250ms. This is an informal indicator and not a clinical diagnosis.
