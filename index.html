Safety Tips
🚗

Use a Rideshare

If your BAC is above 0.05, always use Uber, Lyft, or a trusted sober friend instead of driving. It's not worth the risk.

💧

Alternate with Water

Drinking a glass of water between alcoholic drinks slows alcohol absorption and reduces your peak BAC level.

🍔

Eat Before & During

Food — especially fats and proteins — in your stomach can slow alcohol absorption by up to 50%.

⏰

Time Is the Only Cure

Coffee, cold showers, and food do NOT sober you up. Your liver metabolizes ~0.015% BAC per hour — nothing else changes this.

📱

Pre-Plan Your Ride

Before drinking, designate a driver or save a rideshare app. Making that decision while sober is always safer.

🤝

Look Out for Your Group

Know the signs of alcohol poisoning: unconsciousness, slow/irregular breathing, pale/bluish skin. Call 911 immediately.

⚖️

Legal Limits Vary

The US federal guideline is 0.08%. For drivers under 21, the limit is effectively 0.00%–0.02% in most states. Commercial drivers: 0.04%.
