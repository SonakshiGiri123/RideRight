Safety Tips

Use a Rideshare

If your BAC is above 0.05, use Uber, Lyft, or a sober friend instead of driving. It's not worth the risk.

Time Is the Only Cure

Coffee, water, and food do not lower your BAC. Your liver metabolizes approximately 0.015% per hour — nothing changes that rate.

Alternate with Water

Drinking water between alcoholic drinks slows absorption and reduces peak BAC. It won't sober you up, but it helps pace things.

Eat Before and During

Food — especially fats and proteins — in your stomach slows alcohol absorption and can reduce peak BAC significantly.

Decide Before You Drink

Designate a driver or save a rideshare app before the night starts. Decisions made while sober are better decisions.

Know the Legal Limits

US general limit: 0.08%. Under 21: effectively 0.00–0.02% in most states. Commercial drivers: 0.04%.

Recognise Alcohol Poisoning

Signs include unconsciousness, slow or irregular breathing, and pale or bluish skin. If you see this, call 911 immediately.
