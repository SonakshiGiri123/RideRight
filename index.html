How Fast Are You Right Now?

Tap the moment it turns green. Five rounds. Your average reaction time is then compared against driving safety benchmarks.

Alcohol slows reaction time before you feel impaired. At 0.05% BAC, reaction time increases by up to 40ms. At 0.08%, that gap can exceed 120ms — the difference between stopping in time and a collision at highway speeds.

Sober baseline: approximately 200–250ms. This test is a rough indicator, not a clinical assessment.
